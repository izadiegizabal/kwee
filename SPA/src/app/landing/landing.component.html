This is Kwee

Hi! Welcome to our website! We are a team of Multimedia Engineering students at the University of Alicante and Kwee is our PBL project that we are developing as part as our senior year. This is a educational project so please enjoy it whilst knowing that the data are for showcase purposes.

The aim of the project is to create a web platform for tech professionals, students and businesses of the industry to connect in a really simple, straightforward and specialised way. The platform has two types of users; on the one hand, the professionals and students that are looking for a job, and on the other, the businesses that are offering them.

Kwee Index

The most important feature that set's us apart from the competition is our very own Kwee Index. This score goes from 0 to 100 and represents how trustworthy the user is. Be confident that your next incorporation to the company will be fully competent and similarly, that you will end up working for a really nice business!

Our industry leading Index is calculated based on real opinions by fellow Kwee users among other parameters. You can be assured that you rest in good hands if a candidate or business has a good score.
